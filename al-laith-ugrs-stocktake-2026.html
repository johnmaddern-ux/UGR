What's a UGR?

UGR stands for Unwritten Ground Rules — the unspoken norms that actually govern how things get done around here. Not what's on the wall. What's in the air.

Every workplace has them. They form quietly, over time, through what leaders model — and what they let slide. If the official values are the script, UGRs are the performance. And UGRs always win.

That's why every statement below begins with "Around here…" — finish each sentence with what's actually true, not what should be. The picture that emerges is the culture we're really living.
